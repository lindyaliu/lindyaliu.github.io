Vancouver Fashion Week

Research Insight 1.

Fashion content creators sought more collaborative and paid opportunities. They believed that opportunities for networking with the local fashion scene and a curated platform for discovering and sharing talent would help them succeed.

“I’m having trouble looking for clients of my own, and it’s difficult to create work without the support of a paying client.” — anonymous creator

“I want a platform that is focused, curated, and allows for the discovery and sharing of ideas or talent.” — anonymous creator
