Vancouver Fashion Week

This three-month project employed methods of interaction design — including ethnography, personas, participatory workshops, and prototyping — to provide Vancouver Fashion Week a content-driven website design. My involvement in this project included: developing and conducting research methods, content design of the website, and managing communications between our team and Vancouver Fashion Week.
